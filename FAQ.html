FAQs

1. How can I use the ingredient choice to find recipes?
- You can select the main ingredient that you want/have (i.e. beef, lamb, chicken) then the website will give you plenty of meal pictures for inspiration!
2. Can I get the meal names as well?
- Yes! If you scroll below the image slideshow, there will be a list of meal ideas corresponding to the pictures!
3. How can I obtain recipes for the dishes?
- This site serves mainly to give meal inspiration in different categories of food! Once you have your inspiration, you can search recipes for it!
4. What if I am vegetarian or vegan?
- Have no fear! Our website also has vegetarian and vegan meal inspiration ideas. You can see these on the home page!
